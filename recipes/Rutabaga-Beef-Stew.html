Rutabaga Beef Stew

What a dish!

Rutabagas and beef chuck roast make this stew a hearty and comforting main dish any night of the week

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless beef chuck roast, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2teaspoon salt
  • 1/2teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2cup dry red wine (Optional)
  • 3 cups low-sodium beef broth
  • 2 cups cubed rutabaga
  • 1 medium onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 4 carrots, cut diagonally into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2cup thinly sliced celery
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons herbes de Provence

Steps

  1. Cut beef chuck roast into 3/4-inch pieces. Toss toggether beef, flour, and 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper in a bowl until coated. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add meat and cook unti browned, about 5 minutes
  2. Add red wine (if using) to pot and simmer until alcohol evaporates, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in both, rutabaga, onion, carrots, celery, garlic, bay leaves, herbes de Provence, and remaining salt and pepper. Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, untl meat and vegetables are tender, 30 to 40 minutes. Discard bay leaves.

462 Calories, 31g Fat, 20g Carbs, 25g Protein
