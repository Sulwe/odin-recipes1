Chicken Tortilla Soup

What is Chicken Tortilla Soup?

Chicken tortilla soup is an authentic Mexican dish, traditionally made with chicken broth, tomatoes, garlic, onions, chiles, and fried tortilla strips.

How to make Chicken Tortilla Soup

Making this soup takes only 35 minutes

The recipe will be shown in the lower section of this page

Saute the onions and garlic, the add chili powder, oregano, tomatoes, broth, and water. Allow to simmer, then add corn, hominy, chiles, beans, cilantro, and chicken. Simmer for a few more minutes then serve topped with your favorite garnishes.

What to Serve with Chicken Tortilla Soup

Chicken tortilla soup in great on its own, or garnished with avocado, cheese, green onions, and sour cream. It can also be served alongside crusty bread or a side salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1(28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1(101/4ounce) can condensed chicken broth
  • 11/4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1(15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 large boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 cup whole corn kernels, cooked
  • 1(4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
  • 1/4cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2cup crushed tortilla chips, or to taste
  • 2 medium avocados, sliced, or to taste
  • 1/2cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions, or to taste

Directions

Step 1

Heat oil in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; saute until soft, about 5 minutes. Stir in crushed tomatoes, condensed broth, water, chili powder, and oregano; bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.

Step 2

Stir in black beans, cooked chicken, corn, hominy, chile peppers, and cilantro. Simmer for 10 minutes

Step 3

Ladle soup into individual serving bowls,and top with crushed tortilla chips, avocado slices, Monterey Jack cheese, and green onions.
